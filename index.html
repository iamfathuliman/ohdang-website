Our Story

Born from passion.
Built on flavour.

OhDang. was born in February 2026 with one mission — to bring the best wok-fired Hokkien Mee to Singapore, made with pride as a 100% Muslim-owned establishment.

We believe great food has no boundaries. Our rich prawn-stock broth, smoky wok hei, and house-made sambal are a love letter to Singapore's hawker heritage — now accessible to the Muslim community too.

From food events to your neighbourhood, we're just getting started. One bowl at a time.
